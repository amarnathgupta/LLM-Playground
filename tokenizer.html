🔍 How BPE (Byte Pair Encoding) Works

Step 1

Start with individual characters — l, o, w, e, r

Step 2

Find the most frequent pair and merge — "l"+"o" = "lo"

Step 3

Repeat until the target vocabulary size is reached

Result

Common words = 1 token. Rare words = multiple tokens.
